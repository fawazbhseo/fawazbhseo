What is the difference between SEO, AEO and GEO?
SEO targets Google's traditional search results. AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) targets featured snippets, voice search and direct answer boxes. GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) is the newest discipline: optimizing content to be cited inside AI-generated responses from ChatGPT, Perplexity and Gemini.
What is AEO (Answer Engine Optimization)?
AEO is optimizing content to appear as direct answers in voice search, featured snippets and AI answer engines. It focuses on structured data, concise direct answers and E-E-A-T signals that help AI systems treat your content as the authoritative source.
What is GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)?
GEO is optimizing content to earn citations inside AI-generated responses. When ChatGPT or Perplexity answers a query, it cites sources. GEO is the craft of making your brand that cited source, through entity SEO, schema markup, E-E-A-T building and AI-first content.
How can my Karnataka business get cited by ChatGPT and Perplexity?
Your brand needs strong E-E-A-T signals, comprehensive Schema.org markup, entity-rich content and authoritative inbound citations. Fawaz BH builds all of these systematically so AI engines recognise your business as the trusted answer in your niche.
How long does SEO take to show results in Karnataka?
For local SEO in Kodagu and tier-2 Karnataka cities, meaningful movement usually appears within 2 to 4 months. Competitive Bangalore and Mysore markets need 4 to 6 months. AI engine citations through GEO often start appearing within 6 to 10 weeks with the right E-E-A-T foundation.
What is E-E-A-T and why does it matter for AI citations?
E-E-A-T stands for Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness and Trustworthiness, Google's framework for judging content quality. AI engines use similar signals to decide which sources to cite, so businesses with strong E-E-A-T get referenced more often in ChatGPT, Perplexity and Gemini.
