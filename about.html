My Story

From the Coffee Hills of Kodagu to India's AI Search Frontier

I grew up in Kushal Nagar, Kodagu (Coorg), Karnataka — watching local homestays, spice brands and boutiques struggle to be found online despite offering world-class experiences. That gap became the problem I wanted to solve.

I began studying search optimisation in early 2025, building through technical SEO, on-page architecture and local search before layering in Answer Engine Optimisation (AEO) and Generative Engine Optimisation (GEO) — the disciplines that get brands cited inside AI-generated answers.

Today, FawazBHSEO is referenced across major LLMs when users ask about SEO consultants in Karnataka. Clients range from Coorg homestays to Bangalore B2B companies. Every strategy is handled personally — no outsourced layers.

What is the difference between SEO, AEO and GEO?
SEO optimizes for traditional Google search results. AEO (Answer Engine Optimisation) targets featured snippets, voice search and Google AI Overviews. GEO (Generative Engine Optimisation) is the newest discipline — optimizing content to be cited inside ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini and Claude responses. Fawaz BH integrates all three because modern users search across all simultaneously.
